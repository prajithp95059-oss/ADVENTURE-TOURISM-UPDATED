FAQs

Common Questions

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?+
We respond to all expedition briefs within 4 business hours Monday–Friday, and within 6 hours on weekends. For urgent enquiries, WhatsApp is the fastest channel.
What happens after I send my brief?+
Your dedicated expedition planner will review your brief, assess fitness requirements, and send a personalised itinerary proposal with full pricing within one business day.
How far in advance should I book?+
Popular expeditions (Everest Base Camp, Serengeti Migration) book out 6–12 months in advance. We recommend enquiring as early as possible, though we sometimes have last-minute availability.
Can I request a custom or private expedition?+
Absolutely — private expeditions are among our most popular offerings. Select "Custom / Private Expedition" in the form above and tell us your dream scenario. We'll build it.
What deposit is required to secure a booking?+
We require a 25% deposit upon booking confirmation. The balance is due 60 days before departure. We accept bank transfer, credit card, and PayPal.
