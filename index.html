Common Questions

FAQ

What fitness level do I need for Everest Base Camp?+
Good cardiovascular fitness and prior multi-day hiking experience are recommended. No technical climbing skills needed, but you should be comfortable walking 5–8 hours per day at altitude.
Is travel insurance included in the price?+
Not included, but required. All guests must hold comprehensive adventure travel insurance with helicopter evacuation cover. We can guide you to the right policy.
How small are the expedition groups?+
We cap all expeditions at 12 participants. Most technical climbs are capped at 8 for a better guide-to-guest ratio and lighter environmental impact.
What is your cancellation policy?+
90+ days: full refund minus 5% admin fee. 60–90 days: 50% refunded. Under 60 days: deposit non-refundable, but free date transfers for medical emergencies.
